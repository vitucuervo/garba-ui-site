Mixin responsive breakpoints
  • @include breakpoint("small")

    - From 0 to 767px

  • @include breakpoint("medium")

    - From 768px to 1024px

  • @include breakpoint("large")

    - From 1025px to 1365px

  • @include breakpoint("huge")

    - From 1366px to ∞

General Mixins
  • @mixin clearfix

    - Clear floated elements (should be applied in the container of the floated elements)

  • @mixin css3

    - Crossbrowser css3 properties (Ex: opacity, transform, rotate)

  • @mixin ellipsis-block

    - Ellipsis for block text (only works in chrome)

  • @mixin vertical-scrollbar

    - Styles for vertical scrollbar (only works in chrome)

  • Transitions

    - Mixins for show/hide transitions

  • @mixin fontStyle

    - Set color, font-size and transform the font-size from px to em
